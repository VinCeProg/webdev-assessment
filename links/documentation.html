Documentation

Empowering Students. Supporting Teachers.

We built this platform with one goal in mind: to make student feedback simple, meaningful, and accessible. Instead of relying on paper surveys that get lost or ignored, our digital feedback system gives students a voice — and helps teachers improve based on real classroom experiences. We believe feedback is a two-way street. Teachers guide students daily, and students have valuable insight on what works in the classroom. Through this system, we’re creating a space where that insight is captured, respected, and used for growth.

Our Mission

We strive to inspire and educate through a seamless and engaging platform, ensuring every user feels welcome and informed.

What This Website Does

This platform allows students to fill out simple, structured feedback forms for their classes and teachers. It’s fast, user-friendly, and flexible — whether students want to submit feedback openly or anonymously.

Students can:

  • Share honest feedback on specific subjects
  • Rate how they feel about lessons and teaching styles
  • Suggest improvements or highlight what they loved
  • Choose to submit with or without their name

Teachers and Admins can:

  • Access all submissions in one place
  • Track trends in teaching performance
  • Use feedback for training, reflection, and planning

Privacy & Anonymity

We care about student privacy. Feedback can be submitted anonymously, allowing students to share their honest thoughts without fear. Names are optional, and responses are stored securely.
